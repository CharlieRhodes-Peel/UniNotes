## DeMorgan's Theorems:
-  Can be used to turn OR into AND and visa-versa
- $\bar{(A + B)} = \bar{A} \cdot \bar{B}$
	- "break the line and change the sign"

	- $\therefore A + B = \bar{(\bar{A} \cdot \bar{B})}$
