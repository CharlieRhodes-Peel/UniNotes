# How to find No. Combinations:
### No. Combination = $\frac{n!}{(n-k)! \times k!} = nCk$
where n is the number of people (your options)
and k is the amount of people you can fit into a box (the spaces aloud for the option)
## Example:
- There are 5 candidates for a committee but only 3 spaces, how many possible committees are there?
- NOTICE, doesn't matter the order so **NOT** [[Permutation]]
- In this example: n = 5 & k = 3
- $\therefore$ No. Combinations = $\frac{5!}{(5-3)! \times 3!} = 10$
