### Definition:
- A set contains elements or members, in which the order, size, nor repetition matters
	- {0,1,8} = {1,0,8} = {1,1,1,0,8}

# Set Notation:

#### $x \epsilon X$ = The set $X$ contains element $x$
#### $i \space \centernot\epsilon$ {t,e,a,m} | I is not in the set
# Symbols

## Union:
#### $X \cup Y =$ {$x|x\epsilon X$ & $x \epsilon Y$}:
- Mesh together
[[Associative]] & [[Commutative]]
## Intersection:
#### $X \cap Y=$  {$x|x\epsilon X$ OR $x \epsilon Y$}:
- The middle of the venn diagram (what do they share
[[Associative]] & [[Commutative]]

## Difference:
#### $X - Y=$  {$x|x\epsilon X$ & $x \centernot\epsilon Y$}:
- what elements are in X that are not in Y
Not [[Associative]] & Not [[Commutative]]

## Cross Product:
#### $X \times Y =$ {$(x,y)|x \epsilon X$ and $y\epsilon Y$}
$|X \times Y| = |X| \cdot |Y|$
Not [[Associative]] & Not [[Commutative]]
Cross product comes **BEFORE** sum
Example:
- {0,1} $\times$ {0,1} = {(0,0), (0,1), (1,0), (1,1)}
	- (a,b) is an example of an [[Ordered Pair]]
## Sum:
#### $X + Y=$ {$(x,0)|x\epsilon X$}$\cup${$(y,1)|y\epsilon Y$}
**NOT** [[Associative]] & **NOT** [[Commutative]]
Example:
if $X =$ {$a,b$} & $Y=${$a,c$} then:
- $X+Y =$ {$(a,0), (b,0)$}$\cup${$(a,1),(c,1)$} 
## Compliment:
### $X^C :=$ {$x|x \notin X$}
## Examples:
#### Where: A = {R,G,B,L,P} & B = {M, P, L}
- $A \cup B =$ {R, G, B, L, P, M}
- $A \cap B =$ {P, L}
- $B - A$ = {M}
- $A - B =$ {R, G, B}
