# Simplification Rules !:
- **ADD**ing two rows : $det(B) = det(A)$
- **SWAPPING** two rows : $det(B) = - det(A)$
- **MULTIPLYING** by factor b : $det(B) = b \times det(A)$
- If you have a [[Diagonalization]]ed matrix, the determinate is the product of the diagonal
