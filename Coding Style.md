## For [[Java]] generally:
- Classes
	- UpperCamelCase
- Variables/Methods/Parameters
	- lowerCamelCase
- Constants
	- UPPERCASE
- Be explicit (within reason)
	- avMk (NO)
	- averageMark (YES!)
	- averageMarkOfStudentAttendingUni (NO)

- Comments should explain **blocks of code**

Look into JavaDoc (on cmd) (compiles comments into documentation)
- Might come in useful in the future !

## Most important is to be **consistent and clear** > anything
