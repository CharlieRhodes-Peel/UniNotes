# DARPA Red Balloon Challenge
An example of social mobilisation is the DARPA red balloon challenge, where $40,000 prize or locating 10 weather balloons across the US. A team of ~4,400 people found all 19 balloons in <9 hours. How did they do it?
# Recursive incentive Scheme
$4,000 is allocated to each baloon. The finder (F) gets **half** ($2000). The rest is propagated up the referral tree (pyramid scheme type shi lol)
