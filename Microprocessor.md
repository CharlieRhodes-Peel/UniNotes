# Has:
- [[CPU]], [[RAM]], storage, I/O, timer, etc. are typically separate
- Amount of [[RAM]], storage and I/O can be set at design time
- Higher cost, but more versatile and general-purpose
- Higher performance (when compared to [[Microcontroller]])
	- Higher power
- 64-bit is common
- Typically has a **deep** pipeline ([[Pipelining]])
