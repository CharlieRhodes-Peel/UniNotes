# How to find No. Permutations
- For a [[set]] of UNQIUE elements:
### No. Permutations = $n!$ 
where n is the size of the [[Set]]

### No. Permutations into a box = $nCr \times r!$
where n is the size of the **CANDIDATES**
& r is the size of the **BOXES** the candidates can go in

- For a [[set]] of NON UNQIUE ELEMENTS
### No. Permutations containing NON uniqiue = $\frac{n!}{n_1! \times n_2! \times ... n_r!}$
#DivisionRule

where n is No. all elements
- $n_1$ = number of non unique elements (type 1)
- $n_r$ represents when you have lists of more than one non unique elements

### No. Permutations of *circular* [[set]] = (n-1)!
